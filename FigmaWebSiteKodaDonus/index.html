ABOUT US

Sushi Rice Is Made With Sugar

The sushi rice that is used in sushi making is a little different than the regular white rice. The sushi variety has added sugar, which gives it a slightly sweet taste

This also helps to bring out the flavors of the fish and other ingredients used in sushi rolls.

Sushi rice is made with sugar because it helps to balance out the flavors of the seafood and gives the sushi a sweeter flavor. Without the sugar, sushi would be quite bland.

Additionally, adding sugar to the rice helps to create a sticky texture that is perfect.

Sushi is typically made with white sushi rice, which is a short-grain rice that is sticky and has a slightly sweet flavor. However, you can use any type of rice you like for sushi – just be sure to follow the correct cooking instructions so that it will be sticky enough to form into rolls.
